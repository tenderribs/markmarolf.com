What do I offer?

With the advent of small unmanned aerial vehicles, many new possibilites have emerged. Images and video taken from the air offer a perspective that cannot be matched from the ground. You can use my service as a way of portraying your business or subject of interest from a birds eye view.

Who am I?

I am a student at the Kanti Baden and have been a drone enthusiast for many years. I came across this business model as a friend of mine asked me to film him driving a tractor on his family's farm. Since then, a few years have passed and my drone-arsenal has grown. I've refined my film-making and piloting skills and I think I can change this hobby into a real income source. If you are interested, you can contact me using the form further below.
